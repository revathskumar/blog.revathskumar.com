Here I comes with new Firefox tip to Open your search in a new tab if you are searching from Firefox Search tab.

1. type about:config in addressbar and hit Enter.

2. Click on I'll be careful, I promise!


3. Search for Openintab
 


4. Double click on false to make it true.

Yes, you have done it. Now close the current tab and try for a new search from search box.
